About Mary

Always interested in unique items at a moderate price, Farmhouse Kitchen’s proprietor, Mary Johnson, seeks them out in travels around Arizona and elsewhere. Retirement from active teaching has allowed her to pursue this long-desired dream, and she hopes her customers also share her enthusiasm—not for living in the past but living with the memories, visions and charm of a simpler time.

A peaceful summer day in AZ, where Mary lives

Learn more about Mary in her blog, "At Home in Holbrook"

"Teacher, writer, and international missionary Mary Johnson has settled down at last with her husband, Jeff, in Holbrook, Arizona. In this tiny town, which served as the real life inspiration for the popular animated film, Cars, she keeps busy with writing, hospitality, gardening, and being active at Peace Lutheran Church, where Jeff is the pastor. In this blog, she tells stories of small town life, work, and ministry."
