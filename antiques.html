About our antiques

Sometimes antiques and sometimes replicas, our products are one-of-a-kind items hand selected for those who enjoy reminders of country life. Whether it is an antique teapot, a handmade quilt, or a set of rural-themed Christmas plates, the smiles and satisfaction of our customers mean the most to us. You will rarely see more than a half dozen items advertised at any one time, but they will be truly special!
